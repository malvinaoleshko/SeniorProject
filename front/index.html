Overview

The mission of Organic Transformations is to help people live their best life through proper nutrition and exercise. It is not just about getting buff or losing weight, it is about educating people on what their body needs in order to be healthy and setting them up to live a long and healthy lifestyle.

Dan Lovas with wife Debbie Lovas

Dan Lovas: Martial artist and nutritionist

Dan Lovas is a career martial artist who started his training in 1972 and has Earned black belts in 7 different martial systems. Dan and his wife Debbie own and operate Auburn Martial Arts Center. While training thousands of people over the last 30 years the one thing they have learned is that most people have no idea of what they should eat in general. People would show up to work out and they haven’t eaten in hours, and they didn’t understand why they weren’t able to get through a 15-minute workout. As a master martial artist Dan felt he needed to do more than just train people in martial arts, he needed to teach them a healthy lifestyle that would bring their martial skills to the highest levels possible. In 2004 Dan had some of his own health issues due to food allergies and did research on holistic nutrition to get back on track. Dan has been at optimal Health for the last 15 years and devoted himself to helping others become the healthiest people they can be.
